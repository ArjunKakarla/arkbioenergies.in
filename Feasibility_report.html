A Thriving Market and Policy Tailwinds

The project is strategically positioned to capitalize on India's ambitious Ethanol Blending Program and the rapid growth of the 2G ethanol market, which is actively supported by government policy and subsidies.

India's EBP Programme: Achievement & Targets

India has successfully achieved its 20% blending target five years ahead of schedule, demonstrating a strong commitment to biofuels. The next phase targets a 27% blend by 2030, ensuring sustained demand for ethanol.

National Demand-Supply Gap

India's ethanol demand continues to outpace supply, creating a substantial and predictable market for new producers. The current gap of over 2 billion liters provides a clear entry opportunity for a plant of this scale.

Proven Technology and Feedstock Security

The project's operational viability is assured through a proven process technology from a leading industry partner, complemented by a secure, local feedstock supply chain that mitigates a primary industry risk.

Abundant Feedstock in Ballari

Ballari, known as the "Rice Bowl" of Karnataka, offers a vast and reliable source of rice straw. The plant's annual requirement of 50,000 tons represents only a small fraction of the district's estimated 80% surplus, ensuring long-term supply stability.
