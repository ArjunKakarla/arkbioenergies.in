Market Opportunity

India's commitment to the Ethanol Blended Petrol (EBP) program creates a massive, stable demand for ethanol. The 2G ethanol sector is a key focus for the government, providing a significant growth opportunity for projects like ours that utilize agricultural waste, ensuring energy security while supporting the rural economy.

Air Quality Improvement

Prevents stubble burning of over 50,000 tons of rice straw annually, reducing PM2.5 pollution and avoiding 30,000 tons of CO2 emissions.

Rural Economy Boost

Creates a new revenue stream for over 5,000 farmers in the Ballari region through the buy-back of agricultural waste.

Resource Efficiency

The plant is designed for 80% water recycling and will utilize lignin by-product to meet its own power requirements, ensuring a circular economy.

Risk & Mitigation

A thorough risk assessment has been conducted to identify potential challenges. Proactive mitigation strategies are in place for each identified risk, ensuring the project's stability and long-term success. Click on each risk to view the corresponding mitigation plan.

Feedstock Volatility

Medium/High

Establish long-term MoUs with farmer cooperatives; maintain a 10,000-ton buffer stock; research diversification to other local biomass like cotton stalk.

Technical Yield Drop

Medium/Medium

Engage in a strong technical partnership with a proven technology provider like Praj Industries; conduct extensive pilot testing before full-scale commissioning.

Regulatory Delays

Medium/Medium

Utilize Karnataka's single-window clearance system; engage in pre-consultation with SEIAA and other regulatory bodies; hire an experienced environmental consultant.

Market Price Fluctuation

Low/Medium

Secure fixed-price off-take agreements with Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs); diversify and maximize revenue from by-products.
