Our Technology

Advanced 2G Fractionation

Unlike 1G ethanol which competes with food security, our process utilizes lignocellulosic biomass (rice straw). We use a proprietary steam explosion and enzymatic hydrolysis process to maximize yield.

1

Pre-treatment (Steam Explosion)

Breaks down the rigid lignin structure of rice straw to make cellulose accessible.

2

Enzymatic Hydrolysis

Conversion of cellulose into fermentable sugars using specialized enzyme cocktails.

3

Fermentation & Distillation

Yeast converts sugars to ethanol, which is then distilled to 99.9% fuel-grade purity.
